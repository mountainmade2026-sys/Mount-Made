Our Story

Born in the hills, built for you

Mount Made started with a simple belief: that the freshest, most wholesome groceries should be available to everyone, not just those who live near the farms. We source directly from trusted growers across Manipur's rich highlands.

Every product that reaches your door has been carefully selected, quality-checked, and packed with care. We treat every order as if we are delivering to our own family.

Today, we are proud to serve thousands of households who count on Mount Made for their daily essentials — and we take that responsibility seriously every single day.

Our Values

Principles that guide us

Everything we do is shaped by a commitment to freshness, transparency, and genuine care for our customers and community.

Honest Pricing

No hidden charges, no surprise fees. What you see is what you pay — clear and straightforward every single time.

Community First
